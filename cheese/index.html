Chef's Special

Spicy CHEEEEEEEEEEEESE Omelette

Ingredients

  • 3 large free-range eggs
  • 2 tbsp whole milk or cream
  • ½ cup grated Pepper Jack CHEEEEEEEEEEEESE
  • ¼ cup grated sharp cheddar CHEEEEEEEEEEEESE
  • 1 fresh jalapeño, thinly sliced
  • ½ tsp smoked paprika
  • ¼ tsp cayenne pepper (more if you dare)
  • Pinch of red chili flakes
  • 1 tbsp unsalted butter
  • Salt & black pepper to taste
  • Fresh chives or cilantro, to finish
  • Hot sauce, for serving

Method

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl. Add milk, smoked paprika, cayenne, a pinch of salt, and plenty of black pepper. Whisk vigorously until fully combined and slightly frothy.
  2. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat. Add butter and let it foam, tilting the pan to coat evenly. Once the foam subsides, you are ready.
  3. Pour in the egg mixture. Let it sit undisturbed for 20 seconds, then use a spatula to gently push cooked egg toward the centre, letting raw egg flow to the edges.
  4. When the egg is just barely set but still glossy on top, scatter both CHEEEEEEEEEEEEESEs and jalapeño slices over one half.
  5. Fold the omelette over the CHEEEEEEEEEEEESE side and slide onto a warm plate. The residual heat finishes melting everything into molten, spicy perfection.
  6. Finish with red chili flakes, fresh chives, and a fearless pour of hot sauce. Devour with reverence.
