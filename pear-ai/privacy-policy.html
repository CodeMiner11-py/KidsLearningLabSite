01

Information We Collect

When you use Pear AI, we collect the following categories of information:

Account information

  • Your name, email address, and profile photo from your Google account, provided when you sign in with Google
  • A unique user identifier assigned by Firebase Authentication

Recipe data

  • Recipes you generate, import, or scan — including titles, descriptions, ingredients, steps, nutrition data, and any generated images
  • Folders you create to organise recipes
  • Ratings and favourites you assign to recipes

Community data (optional)

  • If you choose to publish a recipe publicly, your display name, email address, and recipe content become visible to other users
  • Comments and likes you submit on community recipes

We do not collect location data, contacts, browsing history, payment information, health data, or any other sensitive personal information.

02

How We Use It

We use the information we collect solely to provide and improve Pear AI:

  • To authenticate your identity and keep your account secure
  • To save and sync your recipes across your devices
  • To display your name and photo in the app interface
  • To power community features (publishing, comments, likes)
  • To send recipe emails when you explicitly request it
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
We never use your data for advertising. Pear AI contains no ads and we do not share your data with advertisers or data brokers.
03

Camera & Photos

Pear AI requests access to your device camera and photo library for the purpose of scanning ingredients. When you use the Scan feature:

  • The image is captured on your device and sent to our AI processing service to identify ingredients
  • Images are processed to generate a recipe and are not stored permanently on our servers
  • Generated recipe images (created by AI) may be stored alongside your recipe in Firestore

Camera access is entirely optional. You can use Pear to create recipes by typing ingredients without ever granting camera permission.

04

Data Storage

Your data is stored using Google Firebase (Firestore), a cloud database service operated by Google LLC. Data is stored in Google's secure data centres.

  • Authentication is handled by Firebase Authentication
  • Recipes, folders, and community content are stored in Cloud Firestore
  • Data is associated with your Google account UID and is only accessible by you (except content you explicitly publish to the community)

You can review Google Firebase's privacy practices at firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

05

AI Processing

Pear AI uses AI to generate recipes and analyse ingredient photos. This processing is performed by our backend services hosted on Cloudflare Workers, which use large language models to generate recipe content.

  • Recipe generation requests include the ingredients and preferences you provide
  • Ingredient scan requests include the image you capture or upload
  • These requests are processed in real time and are not used to train AI models on your behalf
  • No personally identifiable information is included in AI requests
06

Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party. We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: Google Firebase and Cloudflare, as described above, to operate the app
  • Email delivery: When you use the Send Email feature, your recipe content and the recipient's address are passed to our email delivery service
  • Legal obligation: If required by law, court order, or to protect the safety of users

All service providers are contractually required to use your data only to provide services to Pear AI.

07

Community Features

Pear AI includes an optional Community section where users can publish recipes publicly. Please be aware:

  • Any recipe you publish will display your name, email address, and recipe content to all users of the app
  • Comments you post are visible to all users
  • You can take down your published recipes at any time from within the app
  • Do not publish content containing personal information you do not wish to share publicly
08

Your Rights

You have the following rights with respect to your personal data:

  • Access: You can view all recipes and data stored in your account within the app
  • Deletion: You can delete individual recipes from within the app at any time. To request complete deletion of your account and all associated data, contact us at the address below
  • Correction: You can edit recipe titles, descriptions, and other content directly in the app
  • Portability: Your recipe content can be shared or exported using the app's Share and Print features
  • Withdraw consent: You can sign out of Pear AI at any time from the Settings screen. Signing out stops any further data collection
To request full account deletion, email us at hello@kidslearninglab.com with the subject line "Delete My Pear AI Account." We will process your request within 30 days.
09

Children's Privacy

Pear AI is designed to be family-friendly. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 without verifiable parental consent. Sign-in requires a Google account, which has its own age requirements.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through Pear AI without your consent, please contact us and we will promptly delete that information.

10

Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information:

  • All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS
  • Firestore security rules restrict data access to the owning user account
  • Authentication is handled entirely by Google — we never see or store your password

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
