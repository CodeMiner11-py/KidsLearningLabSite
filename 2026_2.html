📋 Student Code of Conduct & Trip Rules

Violations may result in students being sent home at family expense.

1.Supervision at all times. Stay with your assigned group and Course Leader. No wandering without explicit permission.
2.Curfew. In hotel rooms by designated curfew. Doors remain closed. No hallway socializing after lights-out.
3.Room rules. No students of different genders in each other's rooms at any time.
4.Electronics. Phones silenced during tours, memorials, theater, and instruction. Location-sharing with parents encouraged.
5.Dress code. Closed-toe shoes for all walking days. Nicer outfit required for Broadway theater (May 15).
6.Conduct at memorials. Quiet, respectful behavior at Arlington, Holocaust Museum, 9/11 Memorial, and all war memorials. No photos in restricted areas.
7.Spending money. Students responsible for personal spending beyond allotted amounts. Keep wallets secure in Times Square and busy areas.
8.Bags & security. Minimize bags for Statue of Liberty and Mt. Vernon. All bags stay on coach at Arlington.
9.Medications. All prescriptions must be disclosed. Students may not share medications. OTC meds must be pre-approved by parents.
10.If separated. Stay calm, stay in place. Contact WorldStrides 24/7: 1-800-468-5899. Find uniformed staff.
11.Social media. No real-time location updates, hotel names, or room numbers on public social media.
12.Illness. Report any illness or injury immediately to a chaperone. Do not hide being sick.

🚨 Emergency Procedures

Medical Emergency

1. Call 911 immediately for any life-threatening situation.

2. Notify the nearest Course Leader or chaperone at once.

3. Do not move an injured person unless in immediate danger.

4. WorldStrides 24/7: 1-800-468-5899

Lost / Missing Student

1. Immediately alert all nearby chaperones and Course Leaders.

2. Note last known location, time, and what the student was wearing.

3. Contact venue security. NYC non-emergency: (646) 610-5000.

4. WorldStrides: 1-800-468-5899. Notify parents only after WorldStrides is contacted.

Threat / Safety Concern

Move away calmly. Alert Course Leader immediately. Follow all law enforcement instructions. Pre-designated meetup: nearest hotel lobby or Grand Central main concourse (NYC) / Hyatt Bethesda entrance (D.C.).

Severe Weather

Course Leaders monitor forecasts daily. In severe weather, all outdoor activities are suspended. Move to the nearest indoor facility. Follow Course Leader instructions.
