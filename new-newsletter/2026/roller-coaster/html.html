What's a g-force, anyway?

Standing still, you feel 1 g. On a coaster, that number swings wildly — here's what different moments actually feel like on your body.

1g
Just standing around. Totally normal.
4g
Bottom of a big hill — you'd feel 4x heavier.
−1g
A big drop — the floaty, stomach-drop feeling.
−2g
You'd float right out of your seat without a harness.
