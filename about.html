About Kids Learning Lab™

Welcome to Kids Learning Lab, a podcast created by a 12-year-old named Eshaan Buddhisagar — made by a kid, for kids. This show covers fun, curious topics to help kids learn and explore the world around them through engaging conversations and quizzes inside episodes.

Who is Eshaan?

Eshaan Buddhisagar began this podcast at just 9 years old and has since created over 40 episodes covering fascinating topics like the water cycle, Disney secrets, and cheese. His passion for technology and space drives him to not only create content but also develop apps and write books aimed at inspiring kids to learn coding and science.

What We Do

This show is 100% kid-made—from writing and editing to recording and publishing. Kids Learning Lab encourages young learners to explore science, technology, and fun facts through short, accessible episodes.

In addition to the podcast, Eshaan has published a Python programming book for kids, Python Learning Lab. You can learn more about his projects by contacting us.

Feel free to reach out or explore our Python Learning Lab page to get started with coding.

– Eshaan Buddhisagar, Creator
