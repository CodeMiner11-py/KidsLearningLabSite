How Epicycle works

A quick tour before you dive in — four steps, no jargon required.

1
Draw one or more shapes Click and drag on the canvas. Lift the mouse to finish a shape — draw as many separate strokes as you like.
2
Hit "Transform All" Every shape you drew gets converted into a Fourier series — a stack of rotating circles (epicycles) whose combined motion traces your drawing.
3
Watch it redraw itself Each shape animates independently and in its own color, looping forever. More circles = more detail, but also more compute.
4
Export / Import Save your drawing as plain text (JSON) and paste it back in later — or share it with someone else to load.
