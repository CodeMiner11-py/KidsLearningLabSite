Jupiter Ed Plus Install


Download Extension (.zip)

Step 1: Download the Extension with the button above

Simply click the button to download the file. Click "Keep" if there's a button to.


Step 2: Unzip the file.

Double-click to unarchive (unzip) the file into a folder called eExtension.

Step 3: Page.

In your Chromium-based browser, click the button below.

Open extensions page

If that doesn't work, open the URL in your browser: chrome://extensions

Step 4: Click magic switch

Click the switch in the top-right of the page.


Step 5: Click "Load Unpacked"

Click the Load unpacked button.


Step 6: Select the eExtension folder in your Downloads.

Then, click Open or Select and you should get the extension!

How to Use JupiterEd Plus


It's super easy to use JupiterEd Plus. When in JupiterEd, the first thing you need to do is go to Settings and change the theme to Orange, just once. Don't worry, you can still choose your colors, this is just for theme overlap. Now, whenever you want, you can press "/F" on your keyboard in Jupiter to change the font, and Q to change the color in the color spectrum.
